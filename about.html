About Us

GrainPlus Logistics Ltd.

GrainPlus Logistics Ltd. is a Nigerian-owned agricultural trading company committed to connecting local producers with international markets. Specializing in the export of high-quality agro commodities, we provide end-to-end trade solutions, including warehousing, cleaning, sorting, grading, and logistics, ensuring that Nigerian businesses and farmers maximize their global trade potential.

With deep expertise in both local and international markets, we offer real-time pricing insights, regulatory compliance, and seamless export processes to help our clients succeed in cross-border trade. At GrainPlus Logistics Ltd., we understand the unique challenges Nigerian exporters face and provide tailored solutions to make international trade accessible, efficient, and profitable.


Whether you're a Nigerian farmer, trader, or business owner, our mission is to simplify agro-commodity exports, ensuring fair pricing, premium quality, and reliable delivery to global destinations. With strong industry networks, expert logistics, and a commitment to excellence, GrainPlus Logistics Ltd. is your trusted partner in taking Nigerian agriculture to the world.
